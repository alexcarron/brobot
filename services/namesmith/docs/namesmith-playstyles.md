# Playstyles

## Grinder

A player who is always active that will constantly grind for tokens and characters through mines and refills

## Casual

A rarely active player who ocassionally plays, grabs a quick refill, and sees what they get from mystery boxes. The typically are on for just 10-30 minutes a day

## Hoarder

A player who tries to get as many characters as possible and tries to get all different types of characters. They will maximize the amount of mystery boxes they can get and try to trade in duplicate characters for new characters they dont own.

## Creative Competiter

A player who wants to get the characters they need to make to the name they think is the most clever possible to win the competition. They'll be focusing on getting specific characters and earning votes.

## Strategy Maximizer

This player looks for loopholes and rules that will maximize their token gain and ability to get rare characters. They're looking for ways to maximize the output of this game even if it is too their detriment.
