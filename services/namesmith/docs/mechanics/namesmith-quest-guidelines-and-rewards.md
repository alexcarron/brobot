# Namesmith Quest Guidelines and Rewards
Optional, time-limited mini goals that give players clear, objective challenges to complete in exchange for rewards (tokens, characters, boxes, or perks).
## Guiding Principles
1. Quests should allow some amount of freedom for how players achieve it. It should be an exact set of steps that players have to follow. Multiple players should be able to complete the quest in different, fun, unique ways
2. Quests must feel achievable with effort, not grinding, random chance, or no effort. Begin with easiers quests before progressing in difficulty without making them so easy they are boring or so hard they are frustrating
3. Quests must not be predictable or uninspired (i.e. earn 10 tokens). Add small twists that surprise or inspire curiosity.
4. Make some quests social in nature
5. Quest rewards must always loop back into the main economy and core gameplay loop to provide player's progression. Do not give meaningless rewards.
6. Quests must be programmable such that whether or not a quest has been completed is always objective and not up to interpretation or ambigious (i.e. Make a name that fits a the theme, "Alien")
## Quest Types
### Resource Gathering
Quests for players who love progress, tangiable gains, structure, and measurable success
- Open 5 mystery boxes and collect 3 rare characters.
- Craft a total of 10 new characters this week.
- Sell or trade 3 unwanted characters to clear your inventory.
- Earn 200 tokens through any means.
### Creative
Quests for players that went to be funny, cleverly express themselves, or have good aesthetics
- Create a name that uses at least one emoji
- Add an emoticon to your name
### Social
Quests that encourages interaction, social engagement, and community
- Successfully trade with three different players
- Help a new player by gifting tokens or a character
- Have matching names with another player
### Chaotic
Quests for players who like chaos, randomness, surprise, and unpredictability
- Successfully trade one character you own for 20 characteres without trading back
### Competitive
Quests for players who want to challenge and outperform others
- Have the highest token count by the end of the week
- Complete all quests this week before anyone else
### Strategic
Quests focused on intelligent decision-making and optimization
- Gain 1,000 tokens from a single refill
## Quest Structure
### Daily Quests
Two or three small quests that can be completed in a day. Resets every 24 hours
### Mystery Quest
A hidden quest that is revealed when all rotating daily quests are completed. Provides extra rewards. Resets every 24 hours
### Weekly Quests
One or two quests that take longer and more thought to complete. Resets every 7 days
## Presentation
Every 24 hours, quests are sent in the quests channel with their name, description, and list of rewards. Each one has a button for claiming the quest. When clicked, if it is determined hte player completed the quests, they will receive the rewards and cannot claim the quest again
